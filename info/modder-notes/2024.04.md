- [#4120：修复 #4106 和 #2921，添加 `ItemID.Sets.DuplicationMenuToolsFilter` ](<https://github.com/tModLoader/tModLoader/commit/e5d324ad1ab8396c403f823fad1158d5a878fe7b>)by JackerAcid
